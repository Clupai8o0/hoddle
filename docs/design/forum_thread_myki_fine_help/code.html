Don't panic, Arjun! Since you weren't stopped by an Authorized Officer (Inspector) at the time, you haven't been "fined" yet. The 'Default Fare' is just PTV's way of charging the maximum daily rate because the system doesn't know where you ended your journey.

  • check_circle Go to the PTV website and register your Myki card if you haven't already.
  • check_circle You can apply for a fare adjustment online by explaining the reader didn't register your tap-off.
  • check_circle They are usually very understanding with students, especially if your travel history shows consistent tap-ons.
