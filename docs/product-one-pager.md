# **Hoddle Melbourne**

Product One-Pager

# **1\. TL;DR**

Hoddle Melbourne is a mentorship platform connecting first-year international students in Melbourne with high-achieving mentors who have overcome similar challenges. The platform delivers niche, one-to-many advice on navigating university life, securing opportunities, and achieving academic and career success. By matching students with mentors who share their background and goals, we're solving the critical gap in guidance and awareness that international students face during their crucial first year.

# **2\. Goals**

## **Business Goals**

* Establish Hoddle as the go-to resource for international student success in Melbourne within the first year of launch  
* Build a sustainable mentor network of 50+ verified high-achieving students with specific, trackable achievements  
* Achieve 80% new student satisfaction rate within the first six months  
* Create a self-sustaining community where mentees become future mentors, contributing their own success stories

## **User Goals**

* Gain practical, actionable advice on time management, academic excellence, and job opportunities from mentors with proven track records  
* Access guidance from mentors who understand their specific cultural and situational challenges  
* Build confidence in navigating university systems, career pathways, and personal development  
* Connect with a supportive community of peers facing similar challenges

## **Non-Goals**

* One-on-one tutoring or academic subject-specific help (focus is on holistic guidance, not coursework assistance)  
* Replacing official university support services (we complement, not compete)  
* Serving domestic students or students beyond their first year (maintaining focus on our core audience)  
* Becoming a job placement or recruitment platform

# **3\. User Stories**

## **Primary Persona: Maya \- First-year International Student**

* *As a first-year international student from India*, I want to learn from someone who successfully balanced part-time work with full-time studies so that I can achieve financial independence without compromising my grades.  
* *As someone new to Melbourne*, I want to understand how previous international students secured internships so that I don't miss hidden opportunities.  
* *As a student struggling with time management*, I want proven strategies from high achievers so that I can excel academically while maintaining work-life balance.

## **Secondary Persona: Alex \- High-Achieving Mentor**

* *As a third-year student who overcame first-year challenges*, I want to share my journey so that I can help others avoid the mistakes I made.  
* *As someone passionate about giving back*, I want an efficient platform to reach multiple mentees so that I can maximize my impact without overwhelming time commitment.  
* *As a mentor*, I want recognition for my contributions so that I can build my personal brand and leadership profile.

# **4\. Functional Requirements**

## **Must-Have (P0)**

* Mentor profile system showcasing specific achievements, background, and expertise areas  
* Matching algorithm connecting students with mentors based on goals, background, and field of study  
* Content library featuring mentor advice sessions, success stories, and practical guides  
* Student onboarding flow capturing goals, challenges, and areas where they need guidance  
* Discussion forums organised by topic (academics, career, lifestyle, finance, etc.)  
* Success story submission system for students to share their wins

## **Should-Have (P1)**

* Live Q\&A sessions where mentors address questions from multiple students simultaneously  
* Resource hub with curated links to university services, job boards, and practical tools  
* Progress tracking for students to log milestones and reflect on their growth  
* Email notification system for new content, upcoming sessions, and community updates  
* Mobile-responsive design for on-the-go access

## **Nice-to-Have (P2)**

* Mentor verification badges based on achievement categories (Academic Excellence, Career Success, Leadership, etc.)  
* Peer connection features allowing students to connect with others sharing similar goals  
* Anonymous question submission for sensitive topics  
* Integration with Melbourne university calendars for key dates and deadlines  
* Multi-language support for major international student demographics

# **5\. User Experience**

## **Primary User Journey: New Student Onboarding**

* Student lands on homepage and sees compelling mentor success stories with clear value proposition  
* Student creates account and completes onboarding questionnaire (5-7 questions about background, goals, challenges)  
* System recommends 3-5 mentors whose profiles align with student's needs and background  
* Student browses mentor content (written advice, video sessions, success stories)  
* Student joins topic-specific discussion or submits question for upcoming Q\&A session  
* Student receives personalized content recommendations via email and in-platform notifications  
* Student engages with community, consumes advice, and implements strategies  
* After achieving milestones, student submits their own success story to inspire others

## **Mentor Journey**

* Mentor applies through invitation or referral, submitting achievements and background  
* Platform team verifies credentials and achievements before approval  
* Mentor creates profile highlighting journey, specific wins, and expertise areas  
* Mentor contributes content through written posts, recorded advice sessions, or live Q\&As  
* Mentor receives analytics on reach and impact (views, engagement, student feedback)  
* Mentor earns recognition badges and builds portfolio of mentorship contributions

## **Edge Cases & UI Notes**

* Handle students with multiple overlapping goals by showing cross-functional mentors  
* Provide fallback mentor recommendations if algorithm finds limited perfect matches  
* Moderate discussion forums to maintain supportive, constructive environment  
* Archive outdated content while preserving evergreen advice  
* Ensure mobile interface prioritizes quick content consumption and easy navigation  
* Display mentor availability status for live sessions clearly to manage expectations

# **6\. Narrative**

It's 2 AM and Priya is staring at her laptop, overwhelmed. She just received her first assignment feedback and it's not what she expected. Back home in Mumbai, she was top of her class. Here in Melbourne, everything feels different. The grading system, the academic expectations, even the way professors communicate. She doesn't know who to ask—her parents are 6,000 miles away and don't understand the Australian system, and she doesn't want to bother her new friends who seem to have it all figured out.

She opens Hoddle Melbourne and sees Raj's profile. He's from Delhi, graduated with honors last year, and now works at one of Melbourne's top tech firms. His story sounds familiar: "I bombed my first essay. Got a Pass when I was used to Distinctions. Here's what I learned..." Priya clicks through and finds a 10-minute video where Raj breaks down exactly how he decoded Australian academic writing, including the templates he used and the university resources he didn't know existed.

Two hours later, Priya has watched three mentors share their strategies. She's downloaded a time management template from Sarah (who also worked 20 hours a week while maintaining a 6.5 GPA), bookmarked an upcoming Q\&A about interview preparation, and posted her first question in the "First Semester Struggles" forum. For the first time since arriving in Melbourne, she feels like she has a roadmap.

Six months pass. Priya aces her next assignment using the academic writing framework she learned. She lands a retail job using interview tips from the platform. She's managing her time using strategies from multiple mentors. When she receives her first High Distinction, she knows exactly what to do: she opens Hoddle and clicks "Share Your Success Story." Her post begins: "Six months ago, I was ready to give up. Here's how I turned it around..."

# **7\. Success Metrics**

## **Primary Metrics**

* **Student satisfaction score:** Target 80%+ rating Hoddle as helpful or very helpful  
* **Content engagement rate:** 70% of active students consume at least one piece of mentor advice per week  
* **Success story submissions:** 15% of active students share their own wins within 6 months of joining  
* **Student retention:** 60% of students remain active (visiting platform at least twice monthly) after 3 months

## **Secondary Metrics**

* **Mentor quality score:** Average mentor rating of 4.2/5 or higher  
* **Community growth:** 25% month-over-month growth in new student sign-ups during academic year  
* **Platform stickiness:** Average session duration of 12+ minutes  
* **Referral rate:** 30% of new students join through peer recommendations

## **Learning Metrics**

* Most-consumed content categories (to inform mentor recruitment priorities)  
* Common question themes (to identify content gaps)  
* Time-to-first-engagement (optimize onboarding effectiveness)  
* Student milestone completion rate (track tangible outcomes like "got first job," "improved GPA," etc.)

# **8\. Milestones & Sequencing**

## **Phase 1: Foundation (Months 1-2)**

* Build MVP with core matching algorithm and mentor profile system  
* Recruit and verify 15-20 initial mentors across diverse achievement areas  
* Create content library with 30+ foundational advice pieces  
* Soft launch with 50 beta students from target universities  
* Gather feedback and iterate on user experience

## **Phase 2: Community Building (Months 3-4)**

* Launch discussion forums and Q\&A functionality  
* Host first 3-4 live mentor Q\&A sessions  
* Implement success story submission system  
* Scale mentor network to 40+ verified mentors  
* Reach 200+ active students through campus partnerships and targeted marketing

## **Phase 3: Optimization & Scale (Months 5-6)**

* Refine matching algorithm based on engagement data  
* Launch mobile-optimized experience  
* Implement automated email engagement campaigns  
* Add progress tracking and personalized content recommendations  
* Expand to 500+ students with mentee-to-mentor conversion program  
* Establish metrics dashboard for tracking community health

## **Phase 4: Sustainability (Months 7-12)**

* Launch mentor recognition and badge system  
* Build resource hub with curated external links  
* Develop peer connection features  
* Explore partnership opportunities with university career services  
* Achieve self-sustaining growth through organic referrals and success stories
